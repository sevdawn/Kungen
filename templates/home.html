ALLOW ME TO EXPLAIN ...

In 2014 I learned of a metal band from Gothenburg, Sweden called AVATAR and they very quickly took over our lives. We've seen them over 20 times in 8 different states and they are largely responsible for really kickstarting my hobby of concert photography. I've taken hundreds of pictures, made countless friends, and created memories that will last a lifetime.
